# Dicionario de dados da base escolhida: 

## Features Description
### Demographics
- **age** – Age of student (17–29)  
- **gender** – Male, Female, Other  
- **academic_year** – Year of study (1–4)  

### Academic Factors
- **study_hours_per_day** – Daily study duration  
- **exam_pressure** – Perceived exam-related stress (1–10)  
- **academic_performance** – Performance score (0–100)  

### Mental Health Indicators
- **stress_level** – Overall stress level (0–10)  
- **anxiety_score** – Anxiety intensity (0–10)  
- **depression_score** – Depression level (0–10)  

### Lifestyle Factors
- **sleep_hours** – Average sleep duration  
- **physical_activity** – Hours of physical activity per week  
- **social_support** – Level of emotional/social support (0–10)  

### Behavioral Factors
- **screen_time** – Daily screen usage (hours)  
- **internet_usage** – Total internet usage (hours)  

### Environmental Factors
- **financial_stress** – Financial pressure (0–10)  
- **family_expectation** – Family expectations (0–10)  

### Derived Metrics
- **burnout_score** – Composite burnout index (0–10)  
- **mental_health_index** – Overall mental wellness score (0–10, higher is better)  
- **risk_level** – Burnout category (Low / Medium / High)  
- **dropout_risk** – Predicted likelihood of academic dropout (0–10)
